Active Approach With People

LYON STATE BANK is committed to cultivating and advancing diversity in all forms because we Believe in a wide range of perspectives and talent is crucial to creating a richer culture for our employees and a greater experience for our diversified global client base.

Senior business leaders make sure that all employees in their groups understand why diversity is important for success and equip them with tools and knowledge to foster inclusion. We have made inclusive leadership and management a core leadership competency. Since 2015, more than 2,500 managers and leaders globally participated in Driving Better Decisions, an initiative that helps leaders and managers understand how unconscious bias works and, most importantly, provides concrete tools and guidance on how to take action to mitigate bias in key people and business decisions. As part of this program, we have redesigned key human resources processes to minimize the potential for bias and foster diversity. We hold our businesses accountable for progress in inclusion and diversity. During our Quarterly Business Reviews, we have focused conversations with each employee group/ business about their plans and progress. We report our progress against our inclusion and diversity initiatives regularly to our Board of Directors. As a signatory of HM Treasury's Women in Finance Charter, which seeks to build a more balanced and diverse financial services industry, TEI is committed to achieving 30% female representation in senior management by 2025.

Inclusion & diversity

Inclusion and diversity are key to our success. By fully leveraging our diverse experiences, backgrounds and insights, we can inspire innovation, challenge the status quo and create better outcomes for our people and our clients.

Regulatory Compliance at LYON STATE BANK

At LYON STATE BANK, we take regulatory compliance seriously. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of integrity and transparency to ensure the safety and security of your financial transactions. We want you to have peace of mind when you choose us as your trusted financial partner.

Consumer Protection Regulations:

We strictly adhere to laws and standards governing fair lending practices, transparency, and the safeguarding of customer data.

Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Regulations:

We diligently verify the identity of our customers and monitor transactions to prevent money laundering and fraudulent activities.

Basel III:

We maintain strong capital reserves and adhere to robust risk management practices to meet Basel III standards.

Data Protection and Privacy Regulations:

We prioritize the protection and privacy of your personal and financial information in compliance with relevant data protection laws.

Interest Rate Regulations:

We adhere to laws and guidelines that set the boundaries for interest rates on loans and deposits to ensure fair and ethical lending practices.
