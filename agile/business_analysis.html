Business Analysis (Immersion)

Course outline

Learn more
Courses Skills you will learn Takeaway Who Should Enroll Request information ENROL NOW

In IT organisations ‘Business Analysts’ are often responsible for the gathering the detailed product requirements. Although, in certain IT/custom software companies, there may be an additional role somewhere in product development may be called ‘Business Analyst.’ A problem arises when this IT/custom software model is inserted into a product software organisation/team, which typically has the role of Product Manager responsible for defining the product. This often leads to misunderstanding between the roles and most Product Managers often try to understand who are and whether these people and aren’t they doing essentially the same job as Product Managers / Product Owners? This is especially common if the engineering teams have grown attached to their Business Analysts and lean on them heavily for the detailed Product requirements. This course will help you understand in detail what a Business Analyst does, how they fit into all types of complex structures and teach you all skills required to succeed in the role and what your roles might be in cases where you need to work with a Product team in non-IT/custom software companies.

Skills you will learn

At the end of this course, you should be able to;

Analyse and combine information obtained from various sources or large number of people into coherent business and strategic insights.

Play a central role in aligning the needs of business units with the IT capabilities

Discern perceived and articulate the actual needs of stakeholders

Serve as a “translator” between business and IT

Facilitate communication between stakeholders within your organisation or any project you work on.
