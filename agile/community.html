Welcome to the Communesity

The Agile Pantha Commune-sity is a platform for mentorship, peer to peer learning, networking and insightful events. By enrolling on any of our courses, you are not just getting trained, you are also joining a community of like minded people who are able to support your growth. We share, learn, help you unlock your true potentials and have lots of fun while doing it.

Our community members and cohorts are young urban professionals (Yuppies) who live in Canada and other major cities in North America, Europe and Africa. They are mostly upwardly-mobile professionals, underemployed or actively seeking economic migration to Canada. The community is committed to supporting you through your transition especially by equipping you with in-demand skills.

One thing they all have in common is that they are Product Management and Tech Support Enthusiasts who are actively looking for opportunities in specific career paths.
