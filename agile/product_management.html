Product Management Immersion

Course outline

Learn more
Courses Skills you will learn Takeaway Who Should Enroll Request information ENROL NOW

The highest paid people in Silicon Valley and the tech industry in general are not developers, programers or professionals with Computer Science background. The real rockstars are Product Leaders who help drive the delivery of highly competitive Products and Services. At the end of this course you should be able to drive any idea from inception through product life cycle to growth, make competing business case to secure funding for your products, build product strategy and roadmaps, help coordinate alignment amongst stakeholders, manage delivery process using business agility techniques, lead delivery teams towards delivering great products.

Skills you will learn

At the end of this course, you should be able to;

Take an idea from incubation, through Product Lifecycle.

Manage communication amongst teams and product stakeholders

Make compelling Business Case for securing funding for your product

Support a Product Delivery Team to deliver great product

Draw up a financial model for the product

Build effective product delivery strategy using human-centered design, customer-centricity and enterprise business agility techniques.

Forecast market size and potentials for success for your product

Grow your product from delivery to scale

  • Fireside Chat with a real life rockstar in Product Management landscape.
