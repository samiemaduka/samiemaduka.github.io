It is not enough to learn Scrum or become Scrum Certified. Many who invest in certifications soon discover that holding a certification may get you noticed but could also be a gateway to countless rejection letters. We know that your aim is to land a job in the role. This course will help you understand the Agile Product Delivery ecosystem so that you can have a deeper understanding of the Scrum role within an eco-system rather than in isolation. It goes beyond getting you noticed to helping you distinguish yourself as an informed practitioner of the trade. We do not just prepare you to take the Scrum Certification Test. We equip you to be a competent Scrum Master with all the tools to support high performing teams.

Skills you will learn

At the end of this course, you should be able to;

Explain the relationship between Agile and Scrum and other Agile Frameworks.

Demonstrate clear understanding of the various accountabilities (roles) in the Scrum Framework.

Show clear mastery of how to create transparency around the ability of teams to deliver valuable outcomes to their stakeholders, and resolve impediments that get in the way.

Facilitate various Scrum Events - Sprint Planning, Sprint, Daily Stand Up, Sprint Review and Sprint Retrospectives.

Navigate complex organisational issues that impact of success of Scrum teams.

Understand the difference between Product & Project Mindset and determine where Scrum works best.

Work strategically with Product Managers (Product owners) and Developers and earn respect.

Become that driving force in your team to drive results with Scrum

Be equipped to motivate others to support your Scrum efforts

Get to network with industry Agile professionals

Develop the skills and knowledge to ace your interviews or get noticed at your current job to boost your career.
