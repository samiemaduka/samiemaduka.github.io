Technology continues to enable businesses scale and connect with customers at an unprecedented rate. The profound changes happening as a result of digital technology will play a major role in determining whether most businesses will remain successful at scale. Our Digital Transformation and Innovation program helps businesses access a pool of highly qualified multidisciplinary professionals who will help your organisation to create, manage and research strategic approaches to scaling your business by leveraging technology to enhance your customers' experience and improve your business by bringing together the capabilities needed to help your business (start-up) grow and thrive in the digital age.

Digital Transformation and Innovation program offers your better business opportunities to:
  1. Communicate with and integrate multi-disciplinary expertise related to digital transformation and innovation.
  2. Lead, design and create applications of digital transformation and innovation using current and emerging tools, techniques and technology.
  3. Improve management, technology, communications, information architecture, data science, UX design, and gender / cultural awareness and demonstrate the ability to apply them in practice.
  4. Assess, test and research digital transformation and innovation with sensitivity and awareness around ethics, equity, diversity, business and social impact.
